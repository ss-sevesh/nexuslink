## Hypothesis Statement

If natural language processing (NLP) models' ability to capture nuanced semantic representations is related to their capacity for hierarchical attention mechanisms, then augmenting transformers with geometric deep learning-inspired architectures will significantly improve the robustness of out-of-domain testing for RTE tasks by >20% without sacrificing accuracy.
