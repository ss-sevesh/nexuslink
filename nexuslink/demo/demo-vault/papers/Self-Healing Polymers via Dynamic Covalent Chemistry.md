## Summary

Self-healing polymers employing dynamic covalent bonds based on the Diels-Alder reaction autonomously repair crack propagation damage without external agents, recovering tensile strength through autonomous repair mechanisms.

## Key Findings

- Demonstrates self-healing polymer as a core process.
- Provides evidence for cross-domain analogies with repair and fault-tolerance systems.
