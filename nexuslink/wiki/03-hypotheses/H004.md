## Hypothesis Statement

If the van der Waals forces governing gecko seta adhesion (β-keratin nanostructures, ~10⁻¹⁰ N per seta) and the quantum Casimir effect arising from vacuum fluctuations at nanometre separations are both manifestations of electromagnetic zero-point energy, then synthetic surfaces with seta-density > 10⁸/cm² and tip radii < 5 nm will achieve room-temperature dry adhesion exceeding 100 kPa without applied voltage.
