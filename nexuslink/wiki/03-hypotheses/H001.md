## Hypothesis Statement

If spatial patterns in mammalian gut microbiota (PF14604) are analogous to Markov chains (PF03417), then we should observe a direct correlation between Shannon entropy of alpha diversity (Sα) and the average path length of Markovian processes (L) in microbial ecosystems.

## Suggested Experiments

1. Sequence microbiota from 50 mammals with varying gut anatomy using 16S rRNA; calculate Sα and compare to L for each species via Markov chain analysis
2. Simulate artificial communities on a range of networks (e.g. Erdos-Renyi, Barabasi-Albert) with identical mean degree; measure Sα and L for each topology
3. Analyze spatial autocorrelation in taxonomic abundance patterns using Moran's I statistic to identify correlations between Sα and L
